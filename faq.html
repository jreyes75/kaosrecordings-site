KAOS is a record label established in 2019, specializing in club and festival-ready music. We release Big Room, Trance, and Hardstyle tracks from small to veteran DJs and Producers; getting support from industry-level DJs such as Ummet Ozcan, Maurice West, W&W, and Blasterjaxx.

In 2021, KAOS has established a music production resources arm, dedicated in helping those who want to get into the music industry; providing sample packs, tutorials, and 1:1 sessions between veteran producers and upcoming musicians.
You can buy them by clicking the buy button, or you can subscribe to one of our subscription plans to get our sample packs, sounds, and other resources. An account and a method of payment will be required in order to purchase our items.
We take Visa, MasterCard, Discover, AMEX, and other major card carriers. We also allow PayPal and Bitcoin currencies.
We are always open for people to join our label in designing sounds and samples for our sample packs. You can send in your portfolio, CV, and a link to your work in our 'Contact' page!
